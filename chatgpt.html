Frequently Asked Questions

What is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is an advanced AI language model developed by OpenAI that can understand and generate human-like text. It's trained on diverse internet text and can assist with writing, coding, analysis, creative tasks, and answering questions on virtually any topic.

Is ChatGPT free to use?

Yes, ChatGPT has a free version with access to GPT-3.5. A premium version (ChatGPT Plus) is available for $20/month with access to GPT-4 and additional features. An API is also available for developers with pay-as-you-go pricing.

What's the difference between GPT-3.5 and GPT-4?

GPT-4 is more capable than GPT-3.5 with better accuracy, reasoning, and understanding of complex instructions. It performs better on creative and technical tasks, and can handle longer conversations more coherently.

Can I use ChatGPT for commercial purposes?

Yes, you can use ChatGPT for commercial purposes through ChatGPT Plus or the API. Content generated by ChatGPT can be used commercially, though you should review OpenAI's terms of service for specific guidelines.

What are the limitations of ChatGPT?

ChatGPT has a knowledge cutoff date and cannot browse the real-time internet. It can sometimes generate plausible-sounding but incorrect information. It also cannot perform actions outside of conversation or access external links.

How do I get started with ChatGPT?

Simply visit chat.openai.com and create a free account. You can start using ChatGPT immediately without any credit card. If you want GPT-4 access, upgrade to ChatGPT Plus.
