Key Features

Wizard Mode (Luna)

Follow a step-by-step wizard that helps you go from a simple idea to a structured outline and then a full first draft seamlessly.

Custom Style Training

Upload your previous work to train Moonbeam on your unique voice, ensuring the AI-generated content sounds exactly like you.

AI Smart Editing

Integrated tools to expand paragraphs, rewrite sentences for clarity, or change the tone of entire sections with one click.

Outline to Draft

Create a logical structure for your essay or blog post first, then let the AI fill in the details section by section while maintaining flow.
